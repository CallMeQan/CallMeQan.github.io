> [!Info]
> - If $A_1, ..., A_n$ is a partition of a sample space, then:
> $$
> P(B) = \sum_{i=1}^n P(A_i)P(B|A_i)
> $$
> - With $A_i$ are **mutually exclusive**
> - Basically it is [[Conditional Probability]] but reverse to calculate the low fraction

- Use when $P(A_i)$ and $P(B|A_i)$ are **known**, and the question ask us to calculate the $P(B)$
