## Probability Mass Function
> [!Important]
> - Total of all probability in a set discrete variable is 1:
> $$
> \sum_{x=1}^{n}{p_i}=1
> $$
> - **With $0\leqslant{p_i}\leqslant{1}$**
> - The probability of random variable $x_i$ is $p_i$
> $$
> P(X=x_i) = p_i
> $$
