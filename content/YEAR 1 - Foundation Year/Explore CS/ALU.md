## ALU (Arithmetic and Logic Unit)

- Performs all the arithmetic and logic operation.
	- Arithmetic: **plus, minus**
	- Logic operation: **comparison, larger, smaller, equal,...**
- ALU **cannot** perform *multiplication and division*
